Department of Computer Science

Vision

To catalyze transformation of students into good human beings and competent Computer Professionals

Mission

Imparting Quality Education to meet the needs of profession and society and to provide an environment conducive to innovation, creativity, team-spirit and entrepreneurial leadership in Computer Science

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science was established to meet the demand of well-qualified computer professionals. The Department aims at imparting quality education and strives to quench the thirst for knowledge. The Department offers one Post Graduate Programme viz 2 Years Full Time Master of Computer Applications and three Under Graduate Programmes viz. 3 Years Full Time Bachelor of Science (Computer Science), Bachelor of Science (Information Technology) and Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA). With State-of-the-Art computing facilities, students of the department have access to sufficient high end computing facilities including latest hardware, software and other reprographic facilities. The facilities are adequate to cater to the needs of Research activities. The Department continually ensures that programme objectives are attained and learning outcomes are monitored through mid semester test, quizzes, assignments and live projects.

The Department has a cohesive team of well qualified and vibrant faculty dedicated for imparting quality education which is evident with shining names of the students in the Merit list of affiliating University. Symposiums, workshops, hands-on training on latest tools, skill development program, expert lectures, conferences and seminars are organized by department. Experts from industries with hands on experience regularly share their domain knowledge with the students to meet the requirement of companies. Department organizes regular industrial and educational visits enabling students to get acquainted with professional work culture and to help them groom according to industry requirements. The department has collaboration with Deen Dayal Upadhyay Kaushal Kendra, DAVV, Indore and signed MoU with companies for academic collaborative projects. The students are getting ample opportunities of placements and most of the passed out students are either well placed or have opted for higher studies.



Department of Management

Vision.

To promote entrepreneurial culture and develop managerial and communication skills among the students, with an objective to bridge the gap between industry and academia and match the global quality of professionals.

Mission.

Department of Management is committed to Institute’s Mission of bestowing quality education through nurtured leadership skills in management programmes.

About the Department

The Department of Management was established to match the industry requirement of qualified Management professionals. Hence, the department focuses on management education more than just a curriculum delivery. The department is committed to develop leadership skills amongst students through innovative thinking and activity based learning with deep understanding of fundamentals of business management. The eminent faculty members are committed to impart quality education and all round development of students with innovative teaching pedagogy which has been designed by keeping in mind the industry requirements.

The Department offers three Post Graduate Programmes wiz Master of Business Administration-2 Years Full-time MBA (Dual Specialization), 2 Years Full-time MBA(Financial Administration) and 2Year Full-time MBA (Marketing Management) and a 3 Years Full-time Under Graduate Programme- Bachelor of Business Administration. The students in Master of Business Administration-MBA (Dual Specialization), (Financial Administration) and (Marketing Management), are admitted as per the guidelines issued by Directorate of Technical Education, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh where as the students in Bachelor of Business Administration-BBA, are admitted through the guidelines of Department of Higher Education, Madhya Pradesh. The programmes follow the curriculum designed by affiliated university, Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya, Indore, Madhya Pradesh. The entire course delivery aims at creating business professionals to face the ever changing dynamic environment. Class room lectures by a pool of highly qualified & experienced faculty members, workshops and seminars by renowned industry & academic experts are organized for the students aiming at their holistic development. Regular Industry visits are conducted to give them an insight of the application of the class room learnings in real life. Students are encouraged to undertake social and academic live projects and are actively involved in presentations, simulations and inter-departmental/institute competitions not only as a part of academic learning but also as a sense of commitment towards society. Extra-curricular activities such as Business Plan Competitions, Management Fest- Prabandhotsav, Trade Fairs, Treasure Hunt and Sports competitions are organized on regular basis for students to provide a platform to show cast their off the track talent. Case Development and Analysis Workshops are organized for the Faculty members and students to inculcate the approach of situation based analysis and decision making in management education. Entrepreneurship Development Cell organizes the activities for the students to inculcate the entrepreneurial skills among them and encourages them to undertake their start-ups. Institutional MoUs with Industry like Bajaj Finserve, TallyChamps etc. facilitate enhancement of students’ skills to make them better professionals and getting prepared for the Industry challenges in the times to come.
